About Me

I’m Bendly Jean, and I am the founder of Ribbon Equity. My company focuses on acquiring, funding, and adding value to companies where we recognize imminent growth potential. Whether in business services, information technology, manufacturing, healthcare, or digital assets, we provide capital, connections, expertise, and experience to help middle-market companies write the next chapter of their growth story.

Prior to founding Ribbon Equity, I completed mergers & acquisitions, debt refinances, and analyzed various strategic alternatives for Fith Third Bank, N.A. as a Corporate & Investment Banking professional. My primary field of expertice includes technology, media, and telecommunications. I received my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a focus in Accounting & Finance from Florida A&M University.

My knowlege and experience in this industry also enable appreciate life outside of the office doing things I love with the people I care about. The ideal use of my time on vacation involves traveling around the globe with my family and close friends.
